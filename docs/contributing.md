# Contributing to a Jar

You don't need a wallet or crypto account to contribute to a Jarfi jar.

---

## How it works

1. The jar owner shares a link: `jarfi.xyz/gift/jar-name`
2. You open the link — no sign-up required
3. Choose an amount ($25, $50, $100, $200, or custom)
4. Click **Pay by card**
5. Complete payment via Transak (card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, bank transfer)
6. The funds appear in the jar within minutes

---

## Payment methods

Transak supports 160+ countries and most major payment methods:

- **Credit / Debit card** (Visa, Mastercard)
- **Apple Pay**
- **Google Pay**
- **Bank transfer** (SEPA, faster payments)
- **Local payment methods** (depends on country)

---

## Currencies

You pay in your local currency (USD, EUR, GBP, UAH, and 40+ others). The conversion to USDC happens automatically — you don't need to worry about crypto.

---

## Minimum contribution

The minimum is **$15** per contribution (Transak minimum). There is no maximum.

---

## Is my payment safe?

Yes. Transak is a regulated fiat-to-crypto provider operating in 160+ countries. Your card details are processed by Transak, not stored by Jarfi.

Once the payment completes, funds are sent directly to the jar's smart contract on Solana — the jar owner cannot access them until the unlock condition is met.

---

## Will I see my contribution?

After payment, your contribution is recorded on the Solana blockchain. The jar owner can see all contributions in their dashboard, including amounts and optional messages.
