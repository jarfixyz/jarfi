# Jar Types

Jarfi has four jar types, each with different unlock rules.

---

## 🎯 Goal Jar

**Unlocks when:** The savings goal is reached.

Best for: A specific purchase — a trip, a laptop, a car downpayment.

- Set a target amount (e.g. $2,000)
- The jar locks until that amount is deposited
- Anyone with the gift link can contribute

**Example:** "Japan Trip — Goal $1,500" — unlocks when $1,500 is collected.

---

## 📅 Date Jar

**Unlocks when:** A specific date arrives.

Best for: Long-term savings — a child's 18th birthday, retirement, a future event.

- Set an unlock date (e.g. January 1, 2034)
- Funds are locked until that date
- Contributions and yield accumulate over time

**Example:** "Eva's 18th Birthday — unlocks Jan 2034" — earns yield for 10 years.

---

## 🏁 Goal by Date

**Unlocks when:** The goal is reached OR the date arrives — whichever comes first.

Best for: Saving toward a goal with a deadline.

- Set both a target amount and a date
- Unlocks as soon as either condition is met
- Great for time-sensitive purchases

**Example:** "House downpayment — $20,000 by Dec 2025"

---

## 🎁 Shared Jar

**Unlocks when:** The creator decides — anytime.

Best for: Group collections, birthday gifts, crowdfunding.

- No lock conditions required
- Goal and date are optional (for display only)
- The creator can withdraw at any time
- Multiple contributors, one jar

**Example:** "Office birthday fund" — 20 colleagues chip in, organizer withdraws when ready.
