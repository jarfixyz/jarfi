# Getting Started

## What you need

- A Solana wallet (Phantom or Solflare) — for the **jar owner** only
- A phone or computer — for anyone who wants to **contribute**

Contributors do not need a wallet. They open a link and pay by card.

---

## Step 1 — Install a wallet

Download **Phantom** (recommended) from [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) or **Solflare** from [solflare.com](https://solflare.com).

Set it to **Devnet** during the beta period.

> On Phantom: Settings → Developer Settings → Change Network → Devnet

---

## Step 2 — Connect to Jarfi

1. Go to [jarfi.xyz/dashboard](https://jarfi.xyz/dashboard)
2. Click **Connect wallet** in the top right
3. Approve the connection in Phantom — no transaction, no fee

---

## Step 3 — Create your first jar

1. Click **+ New jar** in the sidebar
2. Choose a jar type (Goal, Date, Goal by Date, or Shared)
3. Enter a name, emoji, and savings goal
4. Set an unlock date (optional, depends on type)
5. Choose who can unlock: Just me or Family approval
6. Review and click **Create**
7. Approve the transaction in Phantom (~0.002 SOL for rent)

Your jar appears in the dashboard immediately.

---

## Step 4 — Fund your jar

After creation, click **+ Add funds** on your jar. This opens Transak — a fiat-to-crypto payment flow. Pay by card, Apple Pay, or bank transfer.

Alternatively, send USDC directly to the vault address shown in the jar details.

---

## Step 5 — Share your gift link

Every jar gets a unique gift link: `jarfi.xyz/gift/your-jar-name`

Share it via WhatsApp, Telegram, or any messaging app. Anyone who opens the link can contribute by card — no wallet required.
